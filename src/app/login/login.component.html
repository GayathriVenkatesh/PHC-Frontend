Our Mission and Vision

Severe acute malnutrition (SAM) is the extreme form of malnutrition responsible for the largest number of preventable death among children under 5 years of age.

Our mission is to work towards Severe Acute Malnutrition free India, through ICT enabled Integrated care.
Our vision is to build seamless community and facility care platforms enabling SAM to be out of the vicious cycle.
