SNEHA PHC

PHC is the basic health unit in a community with a qualified doctor of public sector, entrusted to provide integrated curative and preventive healthcare to its population. They play a major role in diagnosing and providing first line of curative management of a severe acute malnourished (SAM) child.
ASHA is a health acitivist in the community who mobilises and facilitate people in accessing health and health related services including those related to nutrition.
